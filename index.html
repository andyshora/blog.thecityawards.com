It's official: Tommi's Burger London has the Best Burger in London!

31 January 2015
#updates #awards

Thousands of Londoners have been voting for the first City Award, and we're delighted to announce that Tommi's Burger Joint have taken the prize for the #BestBurgerInLondon in 2014!

Tommi's have beaten off some fierce competition for the award, including strong contenters in Patty & Bun and Burger Bear, but the King's Road (and now Marylebone) -based burger joint soon raced into an unassailable lead, taking almost 25% of the votes for the 16 restaurants!
