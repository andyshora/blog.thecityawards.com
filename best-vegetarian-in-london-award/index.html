We've launched the #BestVegetarianInLondon award!

07 February 2015
#updates #awards

Although our launch was famously meat-themed, it's the opposite crowd who have come forth and made sure our second award is in their hands!

The race to find the Best Vegetarian in London is well and truly on. The Gate in Islington have taken quite a commanding lead from the venues on our shortlist, taking in over 50% of the vote. However, voting has just begun, and it's still not too late to nominate your favourite venue if you don't see them on the list.

Remember to tweet your vote with the hashtag #BestVegetarianInLondon for your chance to win a free meal for two at one of the venues this month!

You can carry on voting for the Best Vegetarian in London, as well as lots of other awards, at www.thecityawards.com.
